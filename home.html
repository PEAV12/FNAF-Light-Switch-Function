MEET THE GANG!


Freddy Fazbear

Freddy takes the role of lead singer and overall performer of the band, standing in the center of the stage.

Bonnie

Bonnie is an animatronic rabbit and the guitarist in Freddy's band, positioned at the left side of the stage.

Chica

Chica is an animatronic chicken and the backup singer in Freddy's band, positioned at the right side of the stage.

Foxy

Foxy is a discontinued animatronic pirate fox entertainer. He resides at his own separate stage in the pizzeria.
